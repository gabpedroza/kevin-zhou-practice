How it works (FSRS Algorithm + Points)

This application uses a modified Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler (FSRS) algorithm.

  • Topics: The system tracks your mastery of specific topics from the handouts.
  • Points System: Each problem has a point value.
    • Higher point problems start with a slightly higher "Difficulty" rating.
    • Your dashboard tracks "Points Earned" (sum of points for completed problems).
  • Ratings:
    • Again (1): Complete blackout.
    • Hard (2): Solved with significant effort/errors.
    • Good (3): Solved correctly with normal effort.
    • Easy (4): Solved effortlessly.
